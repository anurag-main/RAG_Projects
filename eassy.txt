Latur is a beautiful city located in the Marathwada region of Maharashtra.

It is known for its rich history, culture, and hardworking people.

The city is famous for its educational institutions and is called the "Oxford of Marathwada."

Latur has developed into a major center for trade and agriculture.

The main crops grown here include sugarcane, soybean, and pulses.

The city also has many sugar factories that support the local economy.

People in Latur celebrate festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi and Diwali with great enthusiasm.
